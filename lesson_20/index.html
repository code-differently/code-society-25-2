Cleveland Bron

LeBron James played in Cleveland from 2003 to 2010 and then again from 2014 to 2018.

Miami Bron

LeBron James played in Miami from 2010 to 2014.

LA Bron

LeBron James played in Los Angeles from 2018 to present.

LeBron's Championships

LeBron has won 4 championships.

  • Miami Heat (2012)
  • Miami Heat (2013)
  • Cleveland Cavaliers (2016)
  • Los Angeles Lakers (2020)
LeBron's Longevity

LeBron has played in the NBA for 22 seasons, and is entering his 23rd season.
