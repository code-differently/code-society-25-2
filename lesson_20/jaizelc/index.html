About Verde Tropical

Our Dominican Heritage

Verde Tropical has been cultivating premium avocados in the fertile soils of the Dominican Republic for over three generations. Our family-owned farms are located in the mountainous regions of Santiago and La Vega, where the perfect climate and rich volcanic soil produce some of the world's finest avocados.

Founded in 1995, we've grown from a small local farm to become one of the leading avocado exporters to the United States, maintaining our commitment to quality, sustainability, and supporting local farming communities.

Premium Avocado Varieties

  • Hass Avocados - Rich, creamy texture with nutty flavor
  • Fuerte Avocados - Smooth, buttery taste with thin skin
  • Reed Avocados - Large, round fruits perfect for families
  • Pinkerton Avocados - Elongated shape with exceptional flavor
  • Bacon Avocados - Mild taste, perfect for first-time avocado lovers

All our avocados are hand-picked at optimal ripeness and carefully processed to maintain freshness during transport to American markets.

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Dominican avocados special?

+

Dominican avocados benefit from our tropical climate, rich volcanic soil, and year-round growing season. Our high-altitude farms in Santiago and La Vega provide the perfect conditions for developing avocados with superior taste, texture, and nutritional content. The combination of mountain air, abundant rainfall, and expert farming techniques passed down through generations creates avocados that are consistently larger, creamier, and more flavorful than many other regions.

How do you ensure freshness during export to the US?

+

We employ a state-of-the-art cold chain system that begins immediately after harvest. Our avocados are carefully hand-picked at optimal ripeness, then quickly cooled and stored in temperature-controlled facilities. We use refrigerated shipping containers maintained at precisely 55Â°F (13Â°C) for the journey to US ports. This process, combined with our relationships with expedited customs brokers, ensures our avocados reach American consumers within 5-7 days of being picked from the tree.

What certifications and quality standards do you maintain?

+

Verde Tropical maintains the highest international standards with USDA Organic certification, GlobalGAP compliance, and Fair Trade certification. We undergo regular inspections by USDA APHIS and work closely with the Dominican Ministry of Agriculture. Our farms follow sustainable practices including integrated pest management, water conservation, and soil health monitoring. Every shipment is accompanied by phytosanitary certificates and undergoes quality testing to ensure it meets or exceeds US import requirements.

Can you supply large retail chains and what are your minimum orders?

+

Yes, we currently supply several major US retail chains and are equipped to handle large-volume orders. Our minimum export order is one 40-foot refrigerated container (approximately 18-20 tons). We can provide consistent weekly shipments year-round due to our multiple farm locations and staggered planting schedules. For retail partnerships, we offer custom packaging, PLU stickers, and can work with your specific ripening and distribution requirements. We also provide marketing support materials highlighting the Dominican origin and premium quality of our avocados.
